About

Avocado Sandwich is a Midwest Emo / Math Rock band formed with a passion for creating emotionally resonant and musically complex arrangements. Since their inception, the band has been dedicated to crafting unique sounds that blend introspective lyrics with intricate instrumentation.

The band's musical journey is characterized by their commitment to artistic expression and innovation. They combine the raw emotion of emo with the mathematical precision of math rock, creating a distinctive sound that resonates with audiences who appreciate both depth and complexity in music.

Through their performances and recordings, Avocado Sandwich continues to push the boundaries of the Midwest Emo and Math Rock genres, establishing themselves as a notable force in the contemporary music scene. Their dedication to their craft and their audience reflects their passion for creating memorable musical experiences.

Each member of the band brings their unique talent and perspective, working together to create music that speaks to the soul while challenging the mind. Avocado Sandwich's music is an invitation to feel deeply and think critically about the world around us.
